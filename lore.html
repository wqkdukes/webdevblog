You may be wondering why the name of this site is squaluslover.me. That is a very good question. Squalus was formerly a genus that every species of shark belonged too. In the modern day this is no longer the case, but it was in 1975 when the film Jaws was released. In that movie the scientist Hooper refers to the shark as a squalus rather than a shark. My friends and I thought that was amusing and since then we've called each other squalus lovers as a joke. Return
